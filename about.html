Hello Again!

Thank you for checking out my portfolio! I am Anuya Karnik, a user-centered designer with zeal for design and development. I am currently a master's student studying Information Science at the University of Michigan, specializing in Human-Computer Interaction and User-Centered Agile Development.

Why User-Centered Design?

I said hello to the world of programming as a computer science major in undergrad while also pursuing a minor a graphic design. While I enjoyed learning the two separately, I wanted to learn more about how the combination of design and development can be used to solve complex problems, leading me to the field of Human-Computer Interaction and UX Design.
