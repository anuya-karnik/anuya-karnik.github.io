My Story

Thanks for checking out my portfolio! I am Anuya Karnik, a user-centered designer who thrives at the intersection of design, tech and business.

Every designer has a unique story, and here is mine. I said "hello" to the world of programming as a computer science major in undergrad while also pursuing a minor a graphic design. While I enjoyed learning the two separately, I wanted to learn more about how the combination of design and development can be used to solve complex problems, leading me to pursue my Master's Degree in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and UX Design.

Now I continue to pursue my passion in the field of User-Centered Design as a UX/UI Consultant at Deloitte.
