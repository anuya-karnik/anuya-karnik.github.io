1Cademy - Practice Tool

1Cademy is a collaborative online community that supports interdisciplinary research and learning through content generation, mapping, evaluation, and practice. It presents information in a graph format which includes connected nodes of knowledge.
The practice tool in 1Cademy provides the users to practice what they have learnt on the 1Cademy platform.
