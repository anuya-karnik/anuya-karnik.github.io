Meatfreed Web Dashboard

The MeatFreed Web App, which provides a dashboard for Business Partners and Restaurant Owners affiliated with our client business, MeatFreed. It supports three types of business users who can manage how end-users interact with promotional offers they have created or are a part of and includes metrics on how MeatFreed end-users engage with these businesses through data such as clicks and views on the customer-facing mobile app.

React.JS, Chart.JS, Firebase, Figma
Software Development in an Agile Environment

About The Client

MeatFreed is a startup that promotes restaurants and businesses that endorse and sell vegan products. Their mobile app provides offers and blog posts to their end-users for vegan restaurants, promoting vegan products and local businesses at the same time.

Problem Statement

Meatfreed business partners lack the visibility of information necessary to help them connect and interact with their customers. Thus, our client wanted a solution that would allow them to increase this visibility and provide their business partners with the right information they need to understand the impact Meatfreed is having on connecting their business or product to customers.

The Solution

To meet our client's need in providing valuable and customized insights to their business partners, we designed and developed the MeatFreed Web App, which provides a dashboard for Business Partners and Restaurant Owners affiliated with MeatFreed. This dashboard:

  • Supports three types of business users who can manage how end-users interact with promotional offers they have created or are a part of.
  • Includes metrics on how MeatFreed end-users engage with these businesses through data such as clicks and views.

Key Features

The following are the key features of the dashboard:

Engagement Data: Each type of user has the ability to view user engagement data associated with their restaurant and offers associated with their business. This data is viewable by graphs over a selected period of time.

View Offers: Users can view and sort offers related to their business and filter offers by the number of clicks. Offers are also updated to indicate the expiry date.

Adding and Updating New Users (Admins Only): Admin Users can add, update, or delete in order to add new businesses and business partners and to business partners in order to add “common users” to manage the account.
